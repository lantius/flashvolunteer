Please take a moment to look at the information below that describe the terms you agree to follow by using Flash Volunteer. We welcome any questions or comments you may have at brad@flashvolunteer.org


At Flash Volunteer, we value your trust. While you may browse some of the website without disclosing personal data, most functionality will only be accessible after creating a profile. The privacy statement describes:

  • What information we collect
  • Why we collect that information
  • Who can see your information

Information collected

We receive and may store any information entered on our website: if you type it in, it will be on our servers. For example, we store information you provide when registering, signing up for an event, creating an event, joining a group, commenting on a profile or an event. This information may include:

  • Your name
  • Your e-mail address
  • Your neighborhood
  • Your work neighborhood
  • Your picture
  • Your interests

We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18 years old and do not collect information about our visitors from other sources such as public records or bodies.

Who can see your information

You are responsible for choosing event visibility settings you are comfortable with. Your options are to share your events with your FlashTeam, everyone or no one. We will honor this choice and only allow parties you have granted visibility to see your events. Anyone who knows your name or e-mail address may see your profile by searching for you.
