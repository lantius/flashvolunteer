Our Mission

Flash Volunteer seeks to build community and increase volunteerism in Seattle through the creation of a sustainable, user-friendly online platform to connect, inspire and mobilize volunteers to effect meaningful change on a neighborhood level. The time has come to create a true, on-line volunteer community that inspires citizens to action and supports their enthusiasm for making a difference. With major budget shortfalls projected in the years ahead and a recent national call for more community service, Flash Volunteer provides the necessary tools to help people make a difference today.
