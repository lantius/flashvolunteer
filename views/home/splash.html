Discover. Connect. Make better.

Flash Volunteer connects Seattle-area residents to volunteer opportunities, volunteers to neighborhoods, and nonprofits to volunteers.

Don't have a lot of time? No problem. When you have some downtime, just visit Flash Volunteer and find a fun and meaningful event in your area that fits your schedule. Maybe even that minute.

  1. Create an account.
  2. Identify the neighborhoods you spend time in and your interests.
  3. Sign up to volunteer for an event.
  4. Attend the event.
  5. Afterwards, become Flash Friends with other event volunteers and find more volunteer opportunities going on nearby.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5, as necessary.
  7. Create and organize volunteer events in your neighborhood.
