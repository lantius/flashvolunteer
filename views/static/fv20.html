Making it easier and more fun than ever to volunteer

2011 was a big year for the development and growth of Flash Volunteer, from a successful partnership with the City of Seattle Department of Neighborhoods to a social innovation award worth $10,000.

So what now?

We are capitalizing on our momentum to boost volunteerism even more in the Puget Sound. We're amping up our outreach and fundraising efforts in support of a revamped platform: Flash Volunteer 2.0.

Flash Volunteer, better than ever

We are looking forward to better mobile functionality and a simpler user interface in the new and improved version of our platform. Among the enhancements we are working on:

For the individual volunteer

  • Easier navigation
  • Ability to add events directly to personalcalendars
  • Event "check-in" on mobile devices
  • Advancements in Flash Volunteer teams
  • Browse for events by organization or cause
  • More customizable profile and user experience
For the volunteer coordinator
  • Quicker event creation
  • Forum for collecting feedback
  • Administrator accounts and organization profile pages
  • Easier social media sharing to broadcast the direct event link
  • Improved notifications of event signups
  • Compile lists of fans by neighborhood
